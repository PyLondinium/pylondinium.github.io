PyLondinium 2019

A Python conference for developers and users of Python

PyLondinium is back for 2019, with a diverse program of sprints, workshops and talks. We'll be returning to Bloomberg's European HQ in the heart of the City of London, June 14th, 15th, and 16th, 2019.

By The Community

PyLondinium is a conference organised volunteers in the City of London. We're looking to provide a gathering opportunity for Python enthusiasts and engineers in London.

For The Community

Tickets are meant to be affordable, and all profits from PyLondinium will go directly to the PSF. We're following the principle of "everyone pays"; everyone including speakers and organisers are required to buy a ticket! Just by coming to the conference, you are helping the wider Python community.
